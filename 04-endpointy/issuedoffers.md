# IssuedOffers

## GET /v1/IssuedOffers

**Issued offers paginated.**

Gets all <b>Issued offers</b> by specified pagination.

---

## POST /v1/IssuedOffers

**Creates Issued offer.**

Creates <b>Issued offer</b> by passed payload.

---

## GET /v1/IssuedOffers/{id}

**Gets Issued offer by id.**

Retrieves <b>Issued offer</b> by passed id, that cannot be empty.

---

## GET /v1/IssuedOffers/{id}/pdf

**Issued offer pdf by id.**

Retrieves <b>Issued offer</b> pdf by id in specified language.

---
