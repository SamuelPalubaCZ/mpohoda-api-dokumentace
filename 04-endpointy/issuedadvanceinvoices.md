# IssuedAdvanceInvoices

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices

**Issued advance invoices paginated.**

Gets all <b>Issued advance invoices</b> by specified pagination.

---

## POST /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices

**Creates Issued advance invoice.**

Creates <b>Issued advance invoice</b> by passed payload.

---

## POST /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/{DocumentId}/Liquidate/Standalone/Fully

**Creates Liquidation.**

Creates <b>Liquidation</b> by passed payload.

---

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/{id}

**Gets Issued advance invoice by id.**

Retrieves <b>Issued advance invoice</b> by passed id, that cannot be empty.

---

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/{id}/isdoc

---

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/{id}/pdf

**Advance invoice pdf by id.**

---

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/{id}/RestToPay

---

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/Liquidation/{id}

## GET /v1/IssuedAdvanceInvoices/Unpaid
