# PriceListItems

## GET /v1/PriceListItems

**Price list items paginated.**

Gets all <b>Price list items</b> by specified pagination.

---

## GET /v1/PriceListItems/{id}

**Gets Price list item by id.**

Retrieves <b>Price list item</b> by passed id, that cannot be empty.

---

## GET /v1/PriceListItems/{PriceListItemId}/RegistrationNumbers

**Registration numbers paginated.**

Gets all <b>Registration numbers</b> by specified pagination.

---

## POST /v1/PriceListItems/AvailableQuantities

**Price list item available quantity by ids.**

Retrieves <b>Price list item available quantities</b> or information about parent entities by passed ids.

---

## POST /v1/PriceListItems/SellingPrices

**Price list item with selling prices by ids.**

Retrieves <b>Price list item with selling prices</b> or information about parent entities by passed ids.

---
