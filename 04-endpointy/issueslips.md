# IssueSlips

## GET /v1/IssueSlips

**Issue slips paginated.**

Gets all <b>Issue slips</b> by specified pagination.

---

## POST /v1/IssueSlips

**Creates Issue slip.**

Creates <b>Issue slip</b> by passed payload.

---

## GET /v1/IssueSlips/{id}

**Gets Issue slip by id.**

Retrieves <b>Issue slip</b> by passed id, that cannot be empty.

---

## GET /v1/IssueSlips/{id}/pdf

**Issue slip pdf by id.**

Retrieves <b>Issue slip</b> pdf by id in specified language.

---
