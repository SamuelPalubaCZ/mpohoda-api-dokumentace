# PaymentMethods

## GET /v1/PaymentMethods

**Payment methods paginated.**

Gets all <b>Payment methods</b> by specified pagination.

---

## POST /v1/PaymentMethods

**Creates Payment method.**

Creates <b>Payment method</b> by passed payload.

---

## GET /v1/PaymentMethods/{id}

**Gets Payment method by id.**

Retrieves <b>Payment method</b> by passed id, that cannot be empty.

---
